This movie is incredibly innovative, it pushes the boundaries of what we expect from mystery fiction and more importantly it challenges our reductive assumptions of animal intelligence.

If you haven’t seen this movie, watch it. It will challenge your opinions and plant a seed of introspection that could blossom into something life changing.
