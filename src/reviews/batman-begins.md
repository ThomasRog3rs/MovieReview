This is the first of the Nolan Batman trilogy. It sets up the origins of Nolan’s Batman, preparing for the best Batman movie ever made to follow on its ground work.

I’m incredibly grateful for this movie because it sets up the universe that allows for the greatest cinematic depiction of the Joker the world has ever seen. However, I do take issue with the main villain in this movie.

The philosophy that drives the motivations of Ras al Gul is fundamentally flawed and displays his ignorance and arrogance. To me, his actions scream out that he’s a narcissistic sadistic destructive man while he hides behind fallacious ethics. 

I feel my read on his character is shown to be correct when he exposes his own contradictions in the reason why he attacks Gotham. He first claims Gotham needs to be destroyed because it has become twisted and corrupt. The city became over ran with criminals due to a massive disparity in wealth distribution leaving many in poverty and starving in the economic crisis. This crisis is something the Wayne family worked hard to resolve with charitable help and even built cheap transport infrastructure for the city and improving the hospitals. Because of the Wayne family, the corrupt city began to recover. Once the Wayne’s were murdered the city slipped back into regression prompting Ras al Gul to formulate his new plan to destroy the city to cleanse the world of criminals. But this is the integral point of my observation: Ras states that it was him who initially caused the financial collapse in Gotham which initially made the city corrupted and ran by criminals. So what was his reason for that?! He brought a city of millions of innocent people too its knees just for the pleasure of watch until he got bored and decided he wanted to burn it down like he did London.

I believe Ras is nothing more than a terrorist rat who virtue signals and hides behind false morality. He’s even more pathetic that the Joker because at least the joke wore his heart on his sleeve, he stood for something. But Ras needed to make excuses to himself to inflict pain. Ras is a flawed person, an intriguing villain and I would love to dive into his back story more.

Rant over. I’M BATMAN
